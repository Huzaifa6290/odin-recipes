Dahi Bhalla

What is this Dahi Bhalla you say !

Dahi bhalla is a type of chaat originating from the Indian subcontinent.

Ingredients

  • 400 gms moong dal
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • salt as required
  • 2 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 Green chilli
  • 3 cups yogurt

Steps

  1. Soak moong dal and urad dal for 2 hours.
  2. Whisk the dal together till it becomes light and fluffy. Add zeera and chironji. The batter is ready.
  3. With wet palms add small portions of batter into hot oil. Deep fry the bhallas till golden brown.
  4. In a blender mix together coriander, mint, green chilli, ginger, black salt and salt.
  5. Soak the tamarind pulp in water for 25-30 minutes. Squeeze it with your hand to extract all the pulp. Strain and thrown away the residue in the strainer.
  6. Mix all the ingredients together.
  7. Sprinkle some chaat masala and serve.
