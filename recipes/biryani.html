BIRYANI

What is this biryani you say !

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of South Asia. It is made with Indian spices, vegetables, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, and fish, or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
  • salt as required
  • 2 tablespoon refined oil
  • 3 green cardamom
  • 2 clove

Steps

  1. soak saffron in water to prepare saffron water.
  2. Now chop the onion and coriander leaves.
  3. Meanwhile, heat refined oil in a deep bottomed and saute for about a minute.
  4. Turn the flame to medium again and add garam masala in it along with ginger julienned, coriander and mint leaves.
  5. Cook for 15-20 minutes with a closed lid and garnish with 1 tbsp fried onions and coriander leaves. Serve hot chicken biryani with raita of your choice. Enjoy!
